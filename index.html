Stay Vertical

Pole dancing studio based in Lowestoft, Suffolk

Our history

Stay Vertical is founded on the shoulders of an incredible woman, Aoife McNeal, who worked in the adult entertainment industry for 10 years, before making her transition into teaching pole dance classes. After 5 years of teaching, she and her husband Andrew opened Stay Vertical (with just 3 poles!) which welcomed its first students through the door in 2015. Since then, the studio has grown and flourished into a 12-pole studio, with a range of pole sizes, materials and spinning settings, and a dedicated area to stretching and conditioning equipment.
